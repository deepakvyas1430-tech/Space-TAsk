Space Tech

How do we explore the universe?

Rockets

Rockets are vehicles specifically designed to travel through outer space. They carry astronauts and satellites by blasting gas out behind them to push forward!

Satellites

These are machines we send into orbit. They help us with GPS, weather forecasting, and taking amazing pictures of deep space.

Rovers

Robotic cars that drive on other planets! Specifically Mars. They take soil samples and look for signs of water.
